Writing Prompts

Prompts are fun exercises that direct you to write about a certain topic to kick start writing inspiration. Here at Maryland Plot Bunny, we provide two types of prompts - question prompts and image prompts. Question prompts are written by us, and are free to use. Images on the image prompts page are public domain images, and will have proper references on the reference page. You can use these prompts in however way you want, to write whatever you want!
