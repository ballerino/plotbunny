Answers:

What is Maryland Plot Bunny about?

Maryland Plot Bunny is a website created to help Maryland writers connect to writing peers in their area by providing lists of writing events and conferences to go to. We want writers to network, share, and become more confident in their writing. All people who call themselves writers are welcome to join, from hobbyists to professional writers!

Who is behind Maryland Plot Bunny?

My name is Megan Tran, and I am the creator of the Maryland Plot Bunny website. I am part of an online Maryland writing community called Plot Bunnies. We decided we wanted a website to gather offline writing events and gatherings to connect with other writers in Maryland.

How often is the site updated?

The website is updated on a Sunday through Saturday weekly basis. The website updates at the beginning of the week on Sunday. We search for new web articles, links, events, and create writing exercises for the website.
back to the top
How do I keep up-to-date with site activities

You can keep updated on our website by signing up for membership to recieve a newsletter each week in your e-mail! You can customize what kind of updates you want as well, such as getting updated on new prompts and events only if you don't care much for writing articles. We send out e-mails near the beginning of the week, and we won't clutter your e-mail! If you ever want to unsubscribe, contact us and we will be happy to help you. Also, take a look at our privacy policy before filling our our forms.

Is the website free to use?

Yes, the website is free to use. You do not need to sign-up to become a member to view our contect. All of our webpages are free to navigate! Feel free to use the writing exercises for your own private use. However, you cannot use anything on this site for commercial use.

I'm not from Maryland, can I still join?

Absolutely! The writing events page is geared towards Maryland writers because we only provide links to Maryland writing events. The rest of the site will include more general creative writing material that anybody can use!
