Privacy Policy

What information do we collect, and when?
We collect information from you when you sign-up for membership and fill out forms on the website. You may be asked to give your name and e-mail address when appropriate, however you can visit and browse the site without registering.

What do we use your information for?
The information we collect from you (names, e-mail addresses) will be used the following ways:
  • To send periodic e-mails (with your permission).
  • To help personalize your e-mail update information (what kind of updates you want from our website).
  • To be able to get in touch with you if you send inquiries.
  • To improve customer service.

How do we protect your information?
We implement a variety of security measures to maintain safety of your personal information.

Do we use cookies?
We do not use cookies.

Do we disclose any information to outside parties?
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer non-personally identifiable and personal information at all. We may release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, protect ours or others rights, property, or safety.

Online Privacy Policy Only
This online privacy policy applies only to information collected through this website and not offline.

Your Consent
