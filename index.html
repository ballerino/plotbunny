Welcome to Maryland Plot Bunny Website!

Today's Date
Welcome to Maryland Plot Bunny, a creative writing website made especially for Marylanders to be able to connect to fellow writers in their area. This website will provide online writing inspiration and exercises, helpful writing links, and lists of writing/reading events throughout Maryland for your convenience. The provided writing prompts and helpful links are here to prepare for any upcoming writing events, and is free to use. Sign up to become a member of our club and recieve updates on our website. Take a look around at our prompts, links, and list of writing events in Maryland. Non-Marylanders are more than welcome to join us and have fun! Any other questions? Feel free to search our FAQ for answers, or contact us!
